Parisian Guide

Find here the best terraces in Paris, without falling into tourist traps!

Our recommendations

Mon Square

Discover the restaurant

Discover a chic and trendy restaurant, located in the heart of Paris, in the 7th arrondissement. You will have the opportunity to eat delicious French cuisine and may even bump into some stars, but shhh, it's a secret!

BB Blanche restaurant

Discover the restaurant

Find this restaurant located in the heart of the 9th district of Paris. Located in a private mansion, you will find a typically Parisian decoration. With a starred chef at the head, you will be able to enjoy refined French cuisine while taking advantage of the very pleasant setting.

Perruche restaurant

Discover the restaurant

Located on the roof of the Printemps department stores', this restaurant offers a breathtaking view of the whole of Paris. You will be able to enjoy good food while taking advantage of the pleasant surroundings. However, plan your visit by booking in advance!

Mun Restaurant

Discover the restaurant

Located next to the Champs-Elysées, enjoy the breathtaking views from this restaurant and the wonderful Asian dishes it offers.

Loulou restaurant

Discover the restaurant

Discover Loulou, the new restaurant located in the Museum of Decorative Arts in the centre of Paris, in the courtyard of the Louvre. You will be able to taste a Mediterranean cuisine revisited by the chef. This restaurant has an ideal location which makes it difficult to access, so think of booking in advance if you want to try it.

Monsieur Bleu restaurant

Discover the restaurant

If you have the opportunity to walk along the quays towards the Palais de Tokyo, you can discover a chic and trendy restaurant with a breathtaking view of the Eiffel Tower. This newly renovated restaurant offers French cuisine with a menu that changes according to the season. It is our favourite in this guide.

Le Brach restaurant

Discover the restaurant

The Brach is a 5-star luxury hotel with a wonderful rooftop where you can enjoy a view of the city of lights from the 16th arrondissement. You will be able to taste a cuisine with Asian flavours but also many pastries.

Café de Flore

Discover the restaurant

Iconic Parisian restaurant, we qualify this café as a must in Paris. Located in the heart of the 6th arrondissement, this café full of history offers a pretty terrace with a diversified menu that will satisfy all tastes. It was the meeting place of many stars (still today). Its flowered terrace will perfectly welcome you for a meal or simply the time to have a drink
